About

Rhythmic

Welcome to our online dance competition platform Rhythmic. We are a team of passionate dancers and dance teachers dedicated to providing dancers from around the world the opportunity to showcase their skills and compete in a fair and inclusive competition. Our platform offers dance courses for various dance styles to help dancers improve their skills and prepare for competitions. Our teachers are experienced professionals who are committed to creating a positive and encouraging learning environment. We provide instructional videos, personal feedback, and customized choreography tips to help contestants improve their performances. Our online dance competition takes place once a month and is open to all dancers regardless of skill, age, or geographic location. Our jury consists of recognized dance professionals who evaluate each performance based on a variety of criteria including technique, creativity, and overall stage presence. We use secure online platforms to receive and judge entries, and our judges must adhere to strict rules and regulations to maintain the integrity of the competition.

In addition to online dance competitions and courses, we specialize in helping dancers build careers in the entertainment industry. Our team of industry professionals works closely with dancers to develop their talents and connect them to opportunities in television, film, and dance choreography. We offer training, support, and networking opportunities to help dancers realize their dreams and succeed in a highly competitive industry. We are pleased to offer our staging services in several locations. Our team of talented dancers and choreographers work together to create stunning performances that leave audiences in awe. Whether you are organizing a corporate event, a wedding, or a cultural festival, we have the expertise to deliver an unforgettable dance performance. At our dance studio, we understand the importance of authenticity and trust in online competitions. That is why we have put strict measures in place to ensure that our competitions are conducted fairly and equitably. Our goal is to provide a positive and encouraging community for dancers of all ages and backgrounds. We encourage dancers of all levels to participate, learn and grow as dancers. Thank you for choosing our online dance competition platform Rhythmic. We are excited to be a part of your dance journey and look forward to seeing what amazing performances our contestants will bring to the competition. Contact us and let's talk more about our dance courses, stage services, and career opportunities.
