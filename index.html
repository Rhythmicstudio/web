About

Rhythmic

Rhythmic is an online dance platform that offers high-quality dance instruction to individuals of all ages and backgrounds. The platform is designed to be accessible and affordable, with free dance classes for children up to 12 years old and affordable classes for those over 12 years old.

Rhythmic offers various dance styles. In addition to dance classes, Rhythmic provides an online dance competition platform where dancers of all ages can showcase their skills and receive feedback from recognized dance professionals.
